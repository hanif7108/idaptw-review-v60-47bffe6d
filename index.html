TL;DR — Ringkas — The IDA-PTW v60 framework achieves composite R² = 0.7091 across 103 spectral periods on 25,058 Indonesian accelerograms under event-grouped 5-fold CV. Stage 0 URPD (AUC = 0.9136) shrinks the near-source blind zone by 71–89%. Stage 1 ensemble (XGBoost + LightGBM, balanced accuracy 81.68%) performs MMI-hybrid 3-class discretisation with Damaging recall ≈ 86%. Stage 2 regressor with intensity-driven adaptive PTW {3, 5, 8}s yields ΔR² = +0.067 over an Atkinson–Boore subduction GMPE baseline. End-to-end latency 10.65 s with 99.44% golden-time compliance. Status: submission-ready, awaiting promotor / co-author review.
