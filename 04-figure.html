Pipeline Produksi Figur

All figures are produced by a single deterministic pipeline (matplotlib 3.10 + cartopy 0.24), with seed-pinned colour maps, embedded LaTeX captions, and PDF/PNG dual output for both manuscript and supplementary. The driver:

Semua figur diproduksi oleh satu pipeline deterministik (matplotlib 3.10 + cartopy 0.24), dengan colour map ber-seed, caption LaTeX terembed, dan output PDF/PNG ganda untuk manuskrip & supplementary. Drivernya: 

cd IDA-PTW/figures
python3 make_all_figures.py --seed 42 --dpi 600 --output ../manuscript/figures/
