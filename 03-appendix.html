Sa(T) ↔ Building Height Correlation

Korelasi Sa(T) ↔ Tinggi Gedung

A natural question raised by the promotor: why predict Sa(T) at 103 periods, and how does period T correlate with building height? The 27-page derivations PDF contains a dedicated subsection mapping the 103 spectral periods to the operational consequence for Indonesian construction stock. The summary mapping:

Pertanyaan natural dari promotor: mengapa prediksi Sa(T) di 103 periode, dan bagaimana periode T berkorelasi dengan tinggi gedung? PDF derivasi 27-halaman memuat subseksi khusus yang memetakan 103 periode spektral ke konsekuensi operasional bagi stok bangunan Indonesia. Ringkasan pemetaan:

Operational implication: the densest portion of the period grid (0.1–1.0 s) aligns with the 90%+ majority of Indonesian occupied building stock per BMKG/PUPR survey 2024. Predicting Sa(T) across this entire band enables targeted alerting per asset class.
