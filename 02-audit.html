Known Minor Notes

Catatan Minor

  1. i18n The Indonesian manuscript source (body_content_v17.tex) uses Mw 5.7 for Sumedang 2024 as historically reported by BMKG initial bulletin; the English source (body_content_EN.tex) uses Mw 5.0 after USGS revision. Both are footnoted with the source authority. Sumber manuskrip Indonesia (body_content_v17.tex) menggunakan Mw 5,7 untuk Sumedang 2024 sesuai bulletin awal BMKG; sumber Inggris (body_content_EN.tex) menggunakan Mw 5,0 setelah revisi USGS. Keduanya diberi catatan kaki sumber.
  2. round All percentages reported to 2 decimal places; raw values in reports/performance/*.csv retain 4 decimals. Semua persentase dilaporkan dengan 2 desimal; nilai mentah di reports/performance/*.csv mempertahankan 4 desimal.
  3. unit Sa(T) values use cgs throughout (cm/s² = gal), consistent with PGA in Table 1 and Equation 14. Nilai Sa(T) menggunakan satuan cgs (cm/s² = gal), konsisten dengan PGA di Tabel 1 dan Persamaan 14.
