06 · SIG-BMKG Intensity Framework

06 · Kerangka Intensitas SIG-BMKG

Indonesia's operational seismic intensity scale — SIG-BMKG (Skala Intensitas Gempabumi BMKG) — is a 5-tier macroseismic scale (I–V) calibrated to InaTEWS public messaging. SIG-BMKG bins correlate strongly with PGA thresholds documented in Wald (1999), Worden (2012), and Caprio (2015), which motivated the MMI-hybrid 3-class boundary used in IDA-PTW Stage 1.

Skala intensitas seismik operasional Indonesia — SIG-BMKG (Skala Intensitas Gempabumi BMKG) — adalah skala makroseismik 5-tier (I–V) yang dikalibrasi ke messaging publik InaTEWS. Bin SIG-BMKG berkorelasi kuat dengan threshold PGA yang didokumentasikan Wald (1999), Worden (2012), dan Caprio (2015), yang memotivasi boundary MMI-hybrid 3-kelas yang digunakan di Stage 1 IDA-PTW.

IDA-PTW MMI-hybrid mapping: the 5-tier SIG-BMKG / 12-tier MMI scale is collapsed to a 3-class supervision target: Non-damaging (< 2 gal) / Felt (2–50 gal) / Damaging (≥ 50 gal) — preserving the operational decision boundary used by InaTEWS to issue earthquake bulletins. Stage 1 ensemble achieves balanced accuracy 81.68% on this 3-class target.

Ensemble Stage 1 mencapai balanced accuracy 81,68% pada target 3-kelas ini.

InaTEWS Operational Integration

Integrasi Operasional InaTEWS

IDA-PTW is designed to integrate downstream of InaTEWS (Indonesian Tsunami Early Warning System), supplementing the existing tsunami-focused pipeline with on-site EEWS capability for direct ground-motion impact. The 4-stage architecture maps to specific InaTEWS operational pipelines:

IDA-PTW dirancang untuk berintegrasi di hilir InaTEWS (Indonesian Tsunami Early Warning System), melengkapi pipeline eksisting yang berfokus tsunami dengan kapabilitas EEWS on-site untuk dampak ground-motion langsung. Arsitektur 4-stage memetakan ke pipeline operasional InaTEWS yang spesifik:
